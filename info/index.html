Security
Symposium AB has appointed Mr Ulf Lindroth as Chief Security Officer (CSO). The CSO is a contracted Security Consultant from one of Sweden’s most highly trusted security companies, 2Secure. The CSO has extensive experience with dignitary protection and a long working history with the Secret Service in Sweden. He spent many years as a personal bodyguard to the royal family and was appointed as a National Security Coordinator when Sweden held the Presidency of the European Union. The CSO has designed the overall Security Plan for the Brilliant Minds Conference in liaison with the Stockholm Police, the City of Stockholm and with all sites that will be visited throughout the program. To ensure your protection, the CSO has contracted bodyguards and accredited stationary guards from G4S Risk Solutions, one of few privately licensed dignitary protection units in Sweden. Mr. Ulf Lindroth and his team will be on duty 24/7.

Important: please note that these security arrangements are designed to cater for the party as a unit. Should individuals choose to stray from the program and explore Stockholm independently, they take personal responsibility for their security and safety needs. Any questions regarding security arrangements should be addressed to Mr. Ulf Lindroth via ulf.lindroth@2secure.se.

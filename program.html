Program

JUNE 8-13 2015

Symposium will feature numerous music, tech and cultural events including the Denniz Pop Awards, Polar Music Prize, Polar Talks, Scandinavian Music Summit, AVICII Fest, and Summerburst, alongside numerous dinners, artist showcases, receptions, festivals and after-parties.

Brilliant Minds Conference

The Brilliant Minds conference is a two-day gathering of some of the greatest minds of our time in music and tech. On June 11-12, participants will explore the evolution of the local and global tech and music industries, bring insight on the latest trends, and forecast future movements. But the real magic is in the unforeseen: the connections, ideas and conversations that the conference will generate.

For further information click here.

Denniz Pop Awards

The Denniz Pop Awards is an initiative by former members of Cheiron Studios and their friends and family in memory of the Swedish DJ, music producer and songwriter Denniz PoP. The goal is to help new songwriters, producers and artists continue the legacy Denniz once started. They do so by every year awarding "Rookie songwriter/producer of the year" and "Rookie artist/band of the year". Through the MVP and Grand Prize Awards, they aim to recognize both the unsung heroes and those who have made an outstanding contribution to promote Swedish music internationally.

For further information click here.

Polar Music Prize

The Polar Music Prize was founded in 1989 by the late Stig "Stikkan" Anderson, one of the true greats in the history of popular music. As the publisher, lyricist and manager of ABBA, he played a key role in their enormous success. Its name stems from Anderson´s legendary record label, Polar Music. The Polar Music Prize is one of the most prestigious and unique music prizes in the world, crossing over musical boundaries and awarded to individuals, groups and institutions in recognition of exceptional achievements.

For further information click here.

Polar Talks

The Polar Talks utilize the unique talents and wide knowledge of the guests gathered in Stockholm for The Polar Music Prize. The Talks have evolved into an international think tank, with a foundation in the creative industries. The event offers a collection of verbal “jam sessions”, where the inspiring presentations cover hot topics like advances in technology, trendspotting and the fascinating stories behind the Polar Music Prize Laureates in a series of panel discussions and debates.

For further information click here.

Scandinavian Music Summit

The Scandinavian Music Summit will take place on June 10-11. All major and indie Scandinavian labels will showcase their latest talent - this is an exclusive chance to have a first look at the next songwriter, artist or band coming out of Sweden, Norway and Denmark. Come to sign your next act, knock off multiple meetings with your peers or simply expand your network.

For further information click here.

Summerburst / AVICII Fest

Summerburst is Scandinavia’s premier contemporary music event. It’s a two-day music extravaganza held in Gothenburg and Stockholm. In 2015 the festival celebrates five years as a leading music event and will welcome music lovers from all over the world. On Friday June 12, Summerburst will become AVICII Fest as the DJ takes centre stage.

For further information click here.

The Brilliant Minds Conference

JUNE 11-12 2015

Founded by Stockholm natives and entrepreneurs Ash Pournouri and Daniel Ek, this exclusive two-day conference will bring together the world’s top innovators, artists, musicians, politicians, CEOs and other top international thought leaders.

  • IMAGESCROLL DOWN

Welcome to the inaugural Brilliant Minds Conference!

This event is invite only.

Participants will explore that which has made Stockholm the creative capital of the world. From Ericsson’s telecom infrastructure to Spotify’s streaming music; from King’s Candy Crush and Mojang’s Minecraft to ABBA and electronic music star AVICII; and so much more… Stockholm companies and artists have been quietly shaping the global future.

The Brilliant Minds Conference will bring together some of Sweden’s legends and her newest talents, along with top

minds globally, to examine the evolution of the local technology and music industries that have shaped culture globally, bring insight into the latest trends, and forecast future movements.

Come be inspired by Sweden’s latest tech, entertained by the nation’s hottest musical exports and connected with some of the brightest and most influential people in the world.
